Got Juice?

Did you used to play horn in school but gave it up after college? Do you want to play drum corps-style music without having to traipse around a football field? Or are you just looking for something fun to do on the weekends?

Brig Juice is a totally free brass ensemble composed of musicians at all different levels. Most of our performances require no memorization or movement and we welcome individuals of all ages (16+). Within the umbrella of Brig Juice, we have many smaller groups and ensembles, ranging from our relaxed and fun Holiday Brass, to our more serious competitive Mini Corps. Whatever your ability or committment level, Brig Juice has a place for you.

Interested in Joining? Contact us at BrigJuice@gmail.com!

In the Spotlight
