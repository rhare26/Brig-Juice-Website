About Brig Juice

Who We Are

Brig Juice Drum & Bugle Corps is a brass ensemble located in Syracuse, NY. It was created in 2015 by Rhys Henson and Bob Marsman and grew out of the Syracuse Brigadiers Drum & Bugle Corps (active 1947-1973 and 1990-2009).

Our organization includes members of all ages and all levels of musicality, from high school students to professionals. Instrumentation consists of trumpets, mellophones, baritones, trombone(s), contras/tubas, and drumset. We perform many kinds of music and throughout a typical year, we'll play everything from Christmas carols to Chick Corea.
